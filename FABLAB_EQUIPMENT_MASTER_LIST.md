## 📦 4. DISK/GENERAL INVENTORY (The "Parts Bin" for Users)

    *   **Resistors:** 1/4W Metal Film Kit (10Ω to 1MΩ).
    *   **Capacitors:** Ceramic (pF to nF) and Electrolytic (1µF to 1000µF) assortments.
    *   **Diodes:** 1N4007 (Rectifier), 1N4148 (Signal), Zeners (various voltages).

    *   **Timers & Op-Amps:** NE555 Timers, LM358 / LM324 Operational Amplifiers.
    *   **Logic Gates:** 74HC series (AND, OR, NOT, Shift Registers like 74HC595).
*   **3. Connectors & Interface Hardware**
    *   **Wiring:** Spools of 22AWG Solid core & Stranded, Silicon high-temp wire.
    *   **Headers:** Male/Female 2.54mm Breakable headers, JST-PH/XH connectors.
    *   **Prototyping:** Breadboards (Full and Half size), Jumper Wires (M-M, M-F, F-F).
*   **4. Power Sources & Management**
    *   **Batteries:** 9V clips, 18650 Li-ion cells + Dedicated Charging Modules (TP4056).
    *   **Adapters:** DC Jack to Terminal block adapters.
